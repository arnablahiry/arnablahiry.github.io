February 2026
Commenced my two-month secondment at the CosmoStat Laboratory (LCS) at the Department of Astrophysics at CEA Paris-Saclay, France, where I continued my work on signal processing and physical inference with high-z galaxy data cubes with deep learning and sparsity, and collaborated with researchers at LCS on related projects and scientific events like seminars and journal clubs.

Contributed a talk at the Deep CosmoStat Days collaborative meeting with researchers from Greece, France and Germany on my ongoing project on beyond moment-0 physical galactic property inference.
September 2025
Participated in the MINOAS – Machine Intelligence for iNverse imaging, Observation Analysis and Sensing Workshop held at FORTH's Institute of Computer Science in Heraklion, Greece, and presented a poster on my work on spectral cube denoising using sparsity and deep learning.
August 2025
Was selected to take part in the Code/Astro workshop on software development for Astrophysics, held at Center for Interdisciplinary Exploration and Research in Astrophysics (CIERA) | Northwestern University, Illinois, USA 🇺🇸, focusing on best practices in scientific software development, version control, and collaborative coding in astrophysics research. I worked on an end-to-end group project with other participants on an astronomical software package.
June 2025
Volunteered as a teaching assistant for the Astrostatistics Summer School 2025 held at the Department of Physics, University of Crete, Heraklion, Greece 🇬🇷, guiding students in practical sessions on statistical methods and machine learning applications in astrophysics.
Attended the UniversAI : Exploring the Universe using Artificial Intelligence - symposium held in Athens, Greece 🇬🇷, and contributed a poster on "Deep and Sparse Denoising of high-z Galaxy Spectral Datacubes - From Simulations to ALMA observations"
March 2025
Began my secondment as a visitng PhD student at the CosmoStat Laboratory (LCS) at the Department of Astrophysics at Commissariat à l'énergie atomique et aux énergies alternatives (CEA), Paris, France 🇫🇷, continuing my work on signal processing of high-z galaxy data cubes with deep learning and sparsity.
July 2024
Attended the second edition of the Machine Learning for Astrophysics (ML4ASTRO) conference, held in Catania, Italy 🇮🇹, and contributed a flash talk on blind source separation and astrophysical signal extraction from IFU spectral data cubes of high-z galaxies
May 2024
Attended the Cosmo21 Conference held in Chania, Greece 🇬🇷, and contributed a poster presentation on the interpretability of CNNs in cosmological inference from large-scale structures of the Universe.
November 2023
Joined the Institutes of Astrophysics and Computer Science at the Foundation for Research and Technology - Hellas (FORTH) and the University of Crete, Heraklion, Greece 🇬🇷, as a PhD student working at the intersection of extragalactic astrophysics and computer science as part of the EU's Horizon 2020 framework programme - ERA chair project.
July 2023
Completed my 5-year Bachelor of Science (BS) - Master of Science (MS) dual degree programme in Physics from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Tirupati, India 🇮🇳, with a Master thesis from CCA, Flatiron Institute, USA 🇺🇸
